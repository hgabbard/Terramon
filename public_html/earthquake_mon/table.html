Expected Seismic Wave Arrival Times for Recent Earthquakes

This site contains data about earthquake events and their projected arrivals at the LIGO observatories in Hanford (LHO) and Livingston (LLO).

This site refreshes once every 30 minutes to get the most recent data.

Guide to this webpage:

  • This list gives expected arrival times of earthquakes at both LIGO interferometers.
  • All clock times are in UTC.
  • Color indicates likelihood of appreciable seismic disturbance at that observatory: Green is low and Red is high.
  • Questions or comments should be addressed to Hunter Gabbard at hagabbar@go.olemiss.edu
